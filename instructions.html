• digital archive is a game in which random words are given to you & you must spell it by catching the falling letters, while also avoiding all the other letters.
  • control the cow with the arrow keys!
  • you will be given a random word and you must spell it by catching the falling letters!
  • but make sure to avoid other letters or else you will lose a life! and you only have three lives! if you spell a word completely and have less than 3 lives, you will get a life back!
  • your points are based off of how many seconds you survive! if you get to 60 points, you move onto level 2!
  • on level 2 the letters drop faster and also sad faces will fall. if you touch a sad face, the game automatically ends!
  • Once you reach 100 points you win! :-)
